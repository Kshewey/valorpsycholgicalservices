Our Clinical Staff

Dr. Nicole Shewey, PsyD

Dr. Shewey is the CEO and owner of Valor Psychological Services. She earned her undergraduate degree at Azusa Pacific University ('08) in Psychology and went on to earn her Master's ('13) and PsyD ('15) from Antioch Unversity New England in Clinical Psychology. Her research interests include multi-cultural issues in psychotherapy, specifically in Asian-American families. She worked for two years as an in-home Applied Behavioral Analyst (ABA) therapist for children on the Autism Spectrum. She specializes in children, teens, and families with emotional dysregulation issues, suicidal ideation, ASD, and personality disorders. She is certified in Functional Family Therapy (FFT) by Western Youth Services and is trained in the ADOS-2. Her therapeutic approach is attachment based, trauma-informed, and influenced by Dialectical Behavior Therapy and FFT.
