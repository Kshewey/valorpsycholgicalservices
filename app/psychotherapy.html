Psychotherapy Approaches

Our primary psychotherapeutic techniques are based on attachment theory. We also integrate many of the techniques from Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Cognitive Behavior Therapy (CBT), and Functional Family Therapy (FFT). â€‹ When clients express that they have concerns about trauma, we are able to utilize treatments such as Trauma-Focused Cognitive Behavior Therapy (TF-CBT), Sand Play Therapy, or aspects of Attachment, Regulation, and Competency (ARC). Clients on the Autism Spectrum typically benefit from interventions such as the Incredible Five Point Scale, Michelle Garcia Winner's Social Thinking Curriculum, and The Zones of Regulation. In addition, we strive to be culturally sensitive in our treatment approach and welcome clients of different faiths, races, and orientations into our practice. Valor Psychological Services.
