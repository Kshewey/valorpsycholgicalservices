Assessment Services

Valor Psychological Services offers psychological assessment for academic, personality, and cognitive testing. Often your provider will recommend this to assist with diagnosis or treatment planning, but at other times, you may be referred to us by another agency or clinician. We do accept most insurance for psychological testing. At this time we are only able to test ages 7 and up, but are planning to test younger ages in the future.
